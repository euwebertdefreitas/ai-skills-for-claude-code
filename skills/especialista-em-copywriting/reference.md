# Expert in Copywriting — Reference

Detailed companion to SKILL.md. Load on demand when a task needs depth beyond the core workflow.

## Key concepts
- **AIDA** — attention, interest, desire, action.
- **Value proposition** — why buy from you.
- **Voice of customer** — their words, not yours.

## Tooling / Stack
- **Frameworks:** AIDA, PAS (problem-agitate-solve).
- **Testing:** A/B, headline tests.
- **Research:** customer interviews, reviews mining.

## Detailed checklists
- [ ] Benefit-led headline
- [ ] Single clear CTA
- [ ] Proof included
- [ ] Concise, customer-voiced

## Common pitfalls
- **Feature-focus.**
- **CTA overload.**
- **Unsupported hype.**

## Further patterns
- **PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve).**
- **Benefit-driven headlines + proof.**
