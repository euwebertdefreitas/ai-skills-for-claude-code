# Expert in Knowledge Management — Reference

Detailed companion to SKILL.md. Load on demand when a task needs depth beyond the core workflow.

## Key concepts
- **Tacit vs explicit knowledge** — know-how vs documented.
- **SECI model** — knowledge conversion.
- **Findability** — taxonomy, search, metadata.

## Tooling / Stack
- **Bases:** wikis, knowledge bases, Notion/Confluence.
- **Structure:** taxonomies, metadata, search.
- **Routines:** after-action reviews.

## Detailed checklists
- [ ] Capture at point of work
- [ ] Structured and searchable
- [ ] Owned with review cycles
- [ ] Reuse measured

## Common pitfalls
- **Content rot** — assign owners.
- **Knowledge silos** — share across teams.
- **Write-only KB** — measure reuse.

## Further patterns
- **Communities of practice.**
- **After-action review (AAR).**
