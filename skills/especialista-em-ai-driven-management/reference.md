- **AI governance** — accountability, bias, transparency.
- **Decision automation tiers** — by risk/volume.

## Tooling / Stack

- **Prediction:** forecasting/ML models.
- **Governance:** audit logs, bias reviews.

- **Accountability gap** — humans stay responsible.
- **Ungoverned bias.**
