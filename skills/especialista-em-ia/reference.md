## Tooling / Stack
- **Classic ML:** scikit-learn.
- **Deep learning:** PyTorch, TensorFlow.

## Detailed checklists
- [ ] Problem genuinely needs AI
- [ ] Data sufficient and representative
- [ ] Metrics, baseline, and oversight defined
- [ ] Ethical/bias review done

## Common pitfalls
- **Hype-driven adoption** — solve real problems.
- **Data leakage** — keep eval honest.
- **Unaccountable automation** — keep humans responsible.

## Further patterns
- **Baseline-first** — beat a simple heuristic before scaling.
- **Human-in-the-loop** for high-stakes outputs.
