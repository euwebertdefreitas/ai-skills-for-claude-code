Detailed companion to SKILL.md. Load on demand when a task needs depth beyond the core workflow.

## Key concepts
- **Skills matrix** — people × competencies × levels.
- **Gap analysis** — current vs required.
- **Capacity/succession planning.**

## Tooling / Stack
- **Mapping:** skills matrices, competency frameworks.
- **Assessment:** self/manager/peer ratings.
- **Planning:** development plans, succession maps.
