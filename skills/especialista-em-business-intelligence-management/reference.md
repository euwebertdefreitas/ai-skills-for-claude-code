# Expert in Business Intelligence Management — Reference

Detailed companion to SKILL.md. Load on demand when a task needs depth beyond the core workflow.

## Key concepts
- **BI CoE** — standards, enablement, governance hub.
- **Metric certification** — trusted, owned definitions.
- **Self-service governance** — guardrails for analysts.

## Tooling / Stack
- **Governance:** semantic layer, metric catalogs.
- **Adoption:** usage analytics on BI tools.
- **Delivery:** agile BI backlog, dbt.

## Detailed checklists
- [ ] Metrics certified and owned
- [ ] Backlog value-prioritized
- [ ] Adoption/usage tracked
- [ ] Stale content retired

## Common pitfalls
- **Shadow BI** — reconcile/absorb rogue reports.
- **Tool sprawl** — standardize platforms.
- **No ROI story** — quantify decisions enabled.

## Further patterns

- **Certified-data tiering.**
